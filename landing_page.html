About us

We are a team endeavoring to help you figure out what path is correct for you in your journey as a Computer Science student.

A Guide for Students

Many students struggle to choose the right course or career path. We wanted to create a resource that offers clear guidance and helps them make informed decisions about their future.

Tailored Recommendations

There is a need for personalized advice in the education sector. Our website provides tailored recommendations based on individual interests and skills, specifically for computer-related courses.

Filling a Gap

We recognized a gap in the available resources for students interested in computer-related fields. By offering a specialized test, we aim to fill this gap and support students in finding the best courses for their aspirations and abilities.

Addressing Uncertainty

We noticed that students often feel unsure when picking computer courses. Our test and personalized advice help clear up confusion, so students can confidently choose courses that match their goals and strengths.
